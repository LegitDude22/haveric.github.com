Instructions

You are the Doctor.

You must get to your T.A.R.D.I.S in order to stop the Dalek invasion.

All you have to use is your trusty sonic screwdriver. Using the sonic screwdriver, you can:

  • Disable (momentarily) Daleks which you are facing.
  • Open/Close the T.A.R.D.I.S doors.

Every so often, a Dalek ship will destroy a city.

You win if you can get inside of the T.A.R.D.I.S.

You lose if all of the cities are destroyed or you get shot by a Dalek.

About

This game was created by Ryan Breuer for Ludum Dare 30.

The theme of Ludum Dare 30 was 'Connected Worlds' which is used in a sense that your actions (or lack of actions) on your world (or moon in this case) directly affect those of the world in the background. (by destroying cities)

I also used a Doctor Who theme for this competition because of the Season 8 premiere that aired during the competition and because who doesn't love Doctor Who?
