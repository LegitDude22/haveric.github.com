About

This game was created by Ryan Breuer for Ludum Dare 35.

The theme of Ludum Dare 35 was Shapeshift. I chose to go with literal shapes shifting in order to consume other shapes of a similar type. The alternate death animation was inspired by the error that occurred shortly after Ludum Dare went live.

Thanks for playing.
